Game Setup

This is a project to host our friend group's rules for our own version of "Kill Your Friends" in DbD. We also randomly generate our builds and the maps we play on to spice up the game we enjoy playing.

Anything that's banned has been removed from the list of things that can be generated. The intent of these rules is to encourage just playing the game for fun and not focus on what's meta or trying too hard.


  • Offerings are not allowed

  • Two maps will be randomly chosen and the survivors will vote on them (Killer breaks a tie)
